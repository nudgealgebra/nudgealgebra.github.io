Introduction

Welcome to NudgeMath. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our app.

Data Collection

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information: Student name, email address and phone number. Parent’s/guardian’s name, email address and phone number.
  • Usage data: This information is collected during registration from the user. It is used for sending reports and updates to the parent.

Use of Data

We use your data to:

  • Send reports of the problems solved by the student to the parent/guardian
  • Update the parent/guardian regarding any programs conducted by the organisation that the student can register for.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure servers.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • - Access your data
  • - Request correction or deletion of your data
  • - Opt-out of data collection, by not registering

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
