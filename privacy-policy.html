Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data

Introduction

Welcome to NudgeMath. Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use NudgeMath.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Student's name, class, and school — collected only with institutional or parental permission.
  • Usage Data: Problem-solving progress, step-by-step work, errors, and completion data — used to provide feedback and track learning.

We do not collect device data such as contacts, location, photos, or browsing activity.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide step-by-step feedback and personalised practice.
  • Generate progress reports for teachers and parents/guardians.
  • Improve content quality and identify common learning gaps.
  • Notify parents/guardians about new features or programs that may benefit the student.

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. We strive to ensure your personal information is safe from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

User Rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt-out of data collection by choosing not to register.

Children's Privacy

NudgeMath is intended for use by students, including those under the age of 13, with the involvement and consent of a parent, guardian, or school. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without appropriate consent. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without proper consent, we will delete such information promptly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page. We encourage you to review it periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
