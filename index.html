Nudge Platform

Designed to support all K12 Math topics

Interactivity Framework

  • - Easy to use touch gestures for each step of the solution
  • - Well thought out interactions that best suit a given topic
  • - Gestures and visual cues designed to reinforce concepts
  • - The worked out solutions look like hand-written answers

Math engine

  • - Tracking the user’s solution at each step
  • - Error determination and categorization
  • - Hint for any given step, based on the user’s solution till that point
  • - Metrics for reports that includes ratings for concept-wise understanding

Nudge Platform

  • - No restrictions on level of complexity of problems
  • - Content agnostic, once designed for a given topic it can be used with any set of problems

For Partners

Nudge platform offerings for partners

Nudge for Schools and Coaching Centers

Teachers can submit problems to Nudge. Students will login for tests and assignments. Teachers receive individual feedback for each student and comprehensive reports.

Benefits

  • - Tailored assignments for each student
  • - Optimal handholding for students during assignments
  • - Zero effort for evaluation

Nudge for Online Learning Platforms

Student logs in to partner app. Learns concepts using partner content. For practice problems, students are seamlessly taken to the nudge platform. Results and metrics are fed back to the parent app.

Benefits

  • - The perfect complement to your concept instructions
  • - A chance to move away from multiple choice questions
  • - Increased engagement of users on the partner solutions

Nudge for Book Publishers

End of chapter problems are fed into Nudge. A companion app is made available to the students buying the book.Students receive guided support for solving the problems.

Benefits

  • - An alternative to solution books
  • - Guided problem solving can be a differentiator
  • - More options for revenue models
